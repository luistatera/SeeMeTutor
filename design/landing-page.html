Features

Built different.
On purpose.

Every design decision comes from one question: what does a truly great human tutor actually do?

🧠

Socratic method — always

Milo never gives the answer. Not even when you beg. It asks questions, highlights what you already know, and leads you to the discovery yourself. The science is clear: you remember what you figure out on your own.

👧
I don't understand why x = 5 here
👁
I can see the equation — what did you do to the left side?
👧
I subtracted 3 from both sides
👁
Exactly right! And what's 8 minus 3?
👁

Sees your actual work

Milo references what's on the page. Not a description — the real thing. "I can see you wrote..." grounds every hint in reality, not guesswork.

🌍

Speaks your language

Milo detects and matches the student's language automatically — no switching, no settings.

✋

Handles interruptions gracefully

Kids interrupt. Thoughts change mid-sentence. Milo stops the moment it hears you, listens, and re-approaches. Just like a real tutor would.

⏳

Infinite patience

No sighs. No judgment. No impatience. Whether it's the first or the fifteenth time you've asked the same question, Milo responds the same way — warmly.
