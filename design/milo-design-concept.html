⑤ Brand Identity
📖
The Notebook Metaphor
Every screen feels like a page. Grid lines, the red margin, the handwriting font — it's school, but warm and alive.
👁
Milo Sees
The eye/lens in the logo is the product promise. Milo watches your work and responds to what it actually sees — not guesses.
💬
Milo's Personality
Patient. Curious. A little playful. Never condescending. Asks questions instead of giving answers. Speaks your language.
